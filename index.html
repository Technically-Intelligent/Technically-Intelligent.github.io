Real vs Artificial Brain Exhibit

This exhibit aims to show how the flow of information works through neurons in the brain, drawing the parallels and differences between what it means to think, create memories, and make choices as a human or machine. Visitors will interact with neural network displays, holographic brain structures, and AI models demonstrating decision-making, biases, and drug impacts.

Why This Exhibit?

Why are we doing this?

AI has saturated pop culture, but the average person doesn’t understand what replicating human thinking means. We want to show how the flow of information works through neurons in the brain, drawing the parallels and differences between what it means to think, create memories, and make choices as a human or machine. Through this exhibit, people will have the ability to interact with neural network displays, holographic brain structure models, and AI models built to show compromised decision-making, biases, and drug impacts.
